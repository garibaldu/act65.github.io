**What happens if we did this?**


$$
\textbf{F}(\textbf{V}) =
\begin{bmatrix}
a & b\\
c & d\\
\end{bmatrix}
\begin{pmatrix}
e & f\\
g & h\\
\end{pmatrix}=
\begin{bmatrix}
a(e)+b(g) & a(f)+b(h)\\
c(e)+d(g) & c(f)+d(h)\\
\end{bmatrix}
$$
